Tony Rustom

1979 - graduated in physiotherapy at Ecole Nationale de Physiothérapie de Mon Seigneur Kortbawi in Lebanon
1980 - specialised in rheumatology at Yverdon Les Bains in Switzerland
1981 - specialised in sports medicine at St Etienne in France, followed by an internship with the football team of St Etienne
1982 -1992 - worked at the orthodox hospital and took in charge the department of physiotherapy
1992 - opened the clinic Centre de Médecine Physique et Sportive Tony Rustom
1999 - graduated for a 2nd time in physiotherapy at Université Libanaise
2000 - specialised in sports medicine from the french school L’INCEP Paris 5 Decartes in Lebanon under the supervision of the trade union
