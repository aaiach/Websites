PARIS KNOCK ON A CLOSED DOOR

Despite completely dominating the match from start to finish, Paris were unable to find a way through a defensive Marseille side on Sunday night at the Parc des Princes. In the end, this 90th Classico in history ended scoreless.

In front of a vocal sell-out crowd, the stage was set for the Classico. And Paris were quickly onto the front foot. As the crowd bellowed ‘Allez Paris’ around the Parc, Les Rouge et Bleu had the ball with 75% possession in the first 15 minutes.

After this Classico that traverses the country, Paris face their shortest away trip of the season in Ligue 1. Next Friday, at 20:45 CET, Paris open Week 11 some 200km north of the capital at the Stade Pierre Mauroy. Against a struggling LOSC, (16th with 10 points), Unai Emery’s men need to bounce back to close the gap to leaders Nice.
